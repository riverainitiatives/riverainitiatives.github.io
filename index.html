November 19, 2023
San Juan, Puerto Rico — In a strategic move to propel molecular biology training and innovation, RIVERA Initiatives Inc. proudly introduces RIVERA-OMICS (Omics Molecular Innovations and Commercialization Strategies), a cutting-edge academic initiative dedicated to advancing the frontiers of molecular biology with a specific emphasis on 'omics' technologies, including genomics, proteomics, and metabolomics.
